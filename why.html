    • Why do it?

    Websites can be made in a lot of ways, you could pay someone else to do it for you, which while it may look good, it would also cost a lot of money and you wouldn’t get the satisfaction of making one on your own. There’s Squarespace/Wix etc. which is a popular choice these days as you don’t need to pay for a domain name and is extremely easy to use, the downsides of using a site like these is that they all have a certain look and feel to them and they aren’t personal. These are the reasons that I chose to learn to make my own websites.
